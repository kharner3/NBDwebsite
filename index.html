Welcome to to the NBD website, where no-bake desserts are #NoBigDeal! This website will teach you how to make a variety of no-bake desserts that use similar ingredients. The recipes are perfect for college students or seniors without access to an oven, children learning to bake, and even adults trying to avoiding heating up the house in the summer.
